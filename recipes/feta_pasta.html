Baked Feta Pasta

Description

An easy weeknight pasta dish that's both fuss free and flavorful. The dish is pretty much hands-off, and all the magic happens in the oven. It's no wonder that this dish went viral in Finland and is now called Tik Tok Pasta!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dried cavatappi pasta, or pasta of choice
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 8 ounce block feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 garlic clovers minced
  • 1/4 cup packed chopped fresh basil plus more for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Place cherry tomatoes in an oven-safe baking dish.
  3. Pour olive oil on top of tomatoes, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss until tomatoes and seasoning are combined.
  5. Place the feta block in the middle of the baking dish surrounded by the cherry tomatoes, and flip a couple of times to coat it with the olive oil and the seasoning.
  6. Bake in oven for 35 minutes, until the cherry tomatoes burst and the feta cheese melts.
  7. While tomatoes and feta are baking, cook the pasta in a pot of salted water according to package instructions until al dente.
  8. Drain water from pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of the water in case you want to add it in.
  9. Add garlic and basil leaves to cooked tomatoes and feta, and toss to combine.
  10. Transfer cooked pasta into baking dish, and toss to combine.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil and serve.
